about us

We will provide you the best work you dreamt for at the most affordable prices.

G.T Handy Projects is a construction company based in Meyerton, Midvaal, Gauteng. We specialize in residential and commercial building projects, renovations, and home improvements. With a strong focus on quality and customer satisfaction, our skilled team is dedicated to delivering exceptional workmanship and service. Established in 2012, we have grown into a trusted construction and maintenance provider, offering reliable services in paving, painting, plumbing, electrical work, welding, landscaping, waterproofing, and general building. Guided by integrity and excellence, we are committed to building lasting client relationships, empowering communities, and growing sustainably.
